Major Arcana

The Fools Journey Through The Arcana

  • Date Published 20.07.01

The major arcana tells the story of the Fool as he travels through the planes of material, mental and spiritual. He is the protagonist of his life and a character we all start out as and relate to.

The Fool (0): Representing the start of our journey full of innocence and ready to take on the challenges on his way.
Upright: A new beginning is coming your way. It is a time to gain expierece in something new and open doors to unfamilar projects. Believe in the universe and take faith in this path
Reversed: Lack of direction and feeling as if stuck in the middle of chaos. A poor decision was made from lack of knowledge.

The Magician (1): Often entwined with the High Priestess, he is conscious awareness. He is the positive side of will and power.
Upright: You have everything you need to reach your full potential or goal. The resources available combined with determination make you unstoppable.
Reversed: There are blocks in communication with a man. Lacking energy and feeling confused from their ill intentions. Lies are coming to the fore.

The High Priestess (2): With the Magician, she is the mysterious unconscious. Our unrelized potential waiting in the realm of creativity.
Upright: Take time to reflect and retreat back from the world. Listen to your inner guide. Things are not how you see it.
Reversed: The full facts are not yet revealed. There is information being withheld causing a lack of peace within you.

The Empress (3): This the fools mother and Mother Earth. She is warm and takes care of him.
Upright: The creation of all things new comes from the Empress. If you starting a family, romance or business she is a sign of this new opportunity.
Reversed: Signifying there are problems at home and a lack of stabily. An unwanted pregnancy or money worries.

The Emperor (4):As the Father, he shows the fool structure and authority. This allows the fool to see purpose in order.
Upright: Things are under control. Hard work and discipline play a big role in your success of taking charge of your life.
Reversed: Manipulation and loss of authority. A strong male character taking advantage of his position.

The Hierophant (5):Stumbling upon organized belief systems, the fool is taught how to interpreet knowledge and mystery from the Priest.
Upright: Education and spiritual beliefs are in the center of your mind. Take guidance from a leader. Marriage may be in your future.
Reversed: Challenging or even rejecting traditional values. Abuse of power or breakdown in order.
